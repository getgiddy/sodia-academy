About SODIA

SODIA serves as a rural-to-global digital talent pipeline, equipping young Nigerians with practical digital skills, certifications, and career opportunities needed to thrive in the global digital economy. The Academy combines practical training, industry-aligned learning, hands-on labs, and measurable employment outcomes to prepare students for opportunities in technology and innovation.

Practical Learning Structure

01.

Hybrid Learning Format

SODIA combines instructor-led teaching with intensive practical learning, creating a balanced training experience focused on both technical understanding and real-world application.

02.

Instructor-Led Sessions

Training is delivered by certified instructors and industry practitioners who guide students through structured learning, technical concepts, and practical exercises.

03.

Intensive Practical Labs

Students participate in hands-on laboratory sessions, simulations, and project-based activities designed to build practical, industry-ready digital skills

04.

70% Practical / 30% Theory

The learning model prioritizes practical experience while supporting technical understanding through focused theoretical instruction.

05.

5 Days Weekly

Students engage in a structured weekly learning schedule that promotes consistency, discipline, and immersive skill development throughout the program.

06.

Morning & Afternoon Sessions

Daily learning sessions are organized across morning and afternoon periods to support intensive training, practical engagement, and collaborative learning experiences.
