Start Your Digital Journey

SODIA offers a rigorous, industry-focused admission process designed for passionate and driven individuals ready to build careers in technology and innovation. Admission is strictly merit-based, ensuring equal opportunity for qualified applicants with strong enthusiasm for digital skills, problem-solving, and professional growth.

Admission Requirements

Applicants seeking admission into SODIA must meet the following minimum requirements:
  • Minimum of Senior Secondary School Certificate (SSCE)
  • Basic computer literacy
  • Minimum age of 16 years
  • Demonstrated proficiency in English
  • Strong interest in technology and innovation

Admission Process

Simple & Transparent Admission Process

SODIA follows a structured and transparent admission process designed to identify motivated and qualified candidates.

  • Online Application Submission
  • Eligibility Screening
  • Aptitude Test
  • Structured Interview
  • Admission Decision

Successful candidates receive official admission offers upon completion of the process.

Scholarships & Sponsorship Opportunities

SODIA operates primarily as a sponsored digital education initiative aimed at expanding access to high-quality technology training. Full scholarships are available through:

  • Corporate Sponsors
  • Title Partners
  • Lab Sponsors
  • Scholarship Partners
Individual Sponsorship

Limited self-sponsored slots are also available for the 6-month intensive program. Tuition fees for self-sponsored students range from ₦450,000 to ₦650,000, depending on the program track and location.


  • Certification examination vouchers may attract additional fees unless fully sponsored.
  • Applicants are strongly encouraged to apply early for scholarship consideration.

‍

Student Commitment
Students are expected to maintain professionalism, academic integrity, active participation, and strong attendance throughout the program.
